FRIENDS FITNESS, YOUR GYM AT YOUR FINGERTIPS

Friends Fitness is a mobile application currently in developpement which will take care of the technological and social aspects found in the modern training routines of today.

Friends Fitness is the technological partner you need to motive and efficiently train you and your friends. Its the new standard for those who wish to keep themselves informed, all while making it easier for you and your friends to work out together.

A home page will offer things such as sports videos, coupons on sporting equipement, progress information regarding your friends and even real time information about your gym.

This app will enable you to

communicate easily with your training center,

socialize with the other members of your gym,

create different training groups for you and your friends.
